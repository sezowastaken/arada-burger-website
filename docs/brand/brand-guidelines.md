# Brand Guidelines

## Visual Direction: Retro-Vintage Burger Diner
The Arada Burger brand is inspired by the classic American diner but modernized for a clean, professional look.

## Core Values
- **Warm:** Inviting colors and friendly layouts.
- **Playful:** Fun mascots and bold typography.
- **Appetizing:** High-quality imagery and a focus on food textures.
- **Bold:** Confident choices in color and type.

## Tone and Feel
- **Professional but Casual:** Like a friendly neighborhood spot that takes its food very seriously.
- **Modernized Retro:** Think classic stripes, badges, and fonts, but with modern spacing and high-quality rendering. Avoid looking "dusty" or outdated.

## Imagery & Assets
- **Mascots:** Use playfully to add character to the menu and landing page.
- **Logos:** Keep visible but balanced.
- **Textures:** Use subtle paper or wood textures to enhance the "diner" feel.

## What to Avoid
- **Clutter:** Don't confuse "retro" with "messy". Keep the layout clean.
- **Over-abstraction:** The food should always be the star. Don't hide it behind too many graphics.
- **Dark/Cold Colors:** Stick to a warm, appetizing palette (Reds, Yellows, Creams, Browns).
