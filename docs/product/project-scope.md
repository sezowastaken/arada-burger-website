# Project Scope

## What is Arada Burger?
Arada Burger is a digital platform designed to bridge the gap between a classic physical burger diner and the modern digital needs of its customers and owners. It is a practical tool, not a complex enterprise system.

## Project Goals
- Provide a beautiful, bilingual digital menu.
- Establish a clear brand identity online.
- Simplify internal business tracking (stock, sales, waste) for non-technical staff.

## What this Project is NOT
- **Not an AI Platform:** No demand forecasting or AI recommendations.
- **Not a Complex Data Engine:** No advanced analytics or "big data" processing.
- **Not Overengineered:** No microservices, no monorepo complexity, no "clever" architectural patterns.

## Current Phase: Phase 1 (Customer Facing)
- **Bilingual Support:** Full Turkish and English content.
- **Digital Menu:** The core product. Needs to be fast and appetizing.
- **Landing Page:** Bold, retro, and welcoming.
- **Essential Info:** Location, About Us, and Contact.

## Future Phase: Phase 2 (Business Operations)
- **Admin Panel:** Simple CRUD operations for products and prices.
- **Manual Data Entry:** Simple forms for staff to log daily sales, waste, and stock levels.
- **User Friendly:** Designed for speed and ease of use in a busy kitchen/office environment.

## Why Simple?
The project prioritizes **maintenance and speed**. It should be easy for a single developer to understand and modify, and it should run reliably on standard, cost-effective infrastructure.
